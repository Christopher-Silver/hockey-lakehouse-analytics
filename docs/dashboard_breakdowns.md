### Shot Miss Map

This dashboard visualizes where players miss the net more or less frequently across the offensive zone.

Key functionality:
- Adjustable minimum shot thresholds to reduce noise
- Spatial binning to highlight shooting tendencies
- Interactive player selection

Primary use cases:
- Identify areas where players struggle to hit the net
- Highlight high-danger shooting zones
- Support coaching decisions around shot selection
